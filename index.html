Anna has been successfully treating professional & amateur sports people in Sussex since she established her business 3 years ago. Anna is a Professional Sports Massage Therapist & Accredited Strength & Conditioning Coach based in Hurstpierpoint. Anna offers a number of Professional Services & Treatments in Sussex. A mobile or home based service is available with daytime, evening and weekend appointments to suit your requirements.

Anna offers the following:

  • Sports Massage Therapy/ Soft Tissue Therapy MSMA (level 4)
  • Sports Injury Treatment
  • Spinal Manipulation
  • Ultrasound therapy
  • Medical Accupuncture/ Dry Needling & Advanced Electro Acupuncture
  • Pregnancy massage (2nd & 3rd Trimester)
  • APPI Clinical/Rehabilitation Matwork Pilates
  • Kinesiotaping® & RockTaping® (Certified Practitioner)
  • Olympic Weight Lifting Coaching BWL Club Coach
  • Strength & Conditioning Coaching (UKSCA Accredited)
  • Athletics- Performance & Development Coaching (UKA level 3)
  • A wide range of complementary therapies including Hot rocks massage & Organic facials
  • Tutoring- UK Athletics, BWL, available for Workshops & seminars

Anna is a professional sports coach specialising in fundamental movements skills, weight lifting, strength & conditioning and athletic performance. Anna currently works with athletes, golfers, racing car drivers, triathletes, hockey players, rugby players, and other amateur and professional sports people across Sussex. Anna has over 14 years of experience as a coach working in professional and amateur clubs, schools, Universities, colleges and independently with children and adults of various abilities. Anna is lead coach at Sussex Strength an Olympic weightlifting facility based at Hurstpierpoint college and runs open weightlifting sessions for juniors & seniors to learn the lifts with prices from just £5. Anna treats & coaches a number of top Sussex athletes that have been to English schools championships and beyond. She coaches the throwing events working with local schools and club athletes. Outside of athletics Anna has a number of clients from Professional golf, Racing car driving, rugby, England hockey, Triathlon amongst others.
